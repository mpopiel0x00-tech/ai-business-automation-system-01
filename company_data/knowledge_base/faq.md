This page answers common questions about using FlowForge.

---

## 1. What is FlowForge?

FlowForge is a workflow management and automation platform designed for teams that need to organize projects, manage tasks, and automate operational processes.

---

## 2. Who is FlowForge designed for?

FlowForge is designed for:

- startups
- agencies
- product teams
- small software companies

The platform helps teams manage projects while automating repetitive operational work.

---

## 3. Can I try FlowForge before purchasing?

Yes. FlowForge offers a free trial period that allows teams to explore the platform before selecting a paid plan.

---

## 4. How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on the number of users (seats) in your workspace. Each active user requires a paid seat.

---

## 5. Is there a limit to the number of projects?

No. All plans allow unlimited projects and task boards.

---

## 6. What are automation rules?

Automation rules allow users to create workflows that automatically perform actions when certain events occur.

Examples include assigning tasks, updating statuses, or sending notifications.

---

## 7. Does FlowForge support integrations with other tools?

Yes. FlowForge integrates with several productivity and development tools including Slack, GitHub, Google Workspace, Zapier, and Notion.

---

## 8. Can I import data from other tools?

Yes. FlowForge provides import tools and API access to migrate tasks and workflows from other platforms.

---

## 9. Is there a mobile app?

FlowForge currently provides a mobile-friendly web interface. Native mobile applications are planned for future releases.

---

## 10. Does FlowForge include AI features?

Yes. FlowForge includes AI-powered task summarization and project insights that help teams understand project progress quickly.

---

## 11. Can I control team permissions?

Yes. FlowForge supports role-based permissions that allow administrators to control access to projects, workflows, and workspace settings.

---

## 12. How secure is FlowForge?

FlowForge includes encryption, audit logging, and enterprise security features to ensure customer data is protected.

More information can be found in the security documentation.

---

## 13. What support options are available?

Support depends on the pricing plan:

Starter includes standard email support.  
Growth includes priority email support.  
Enterprise includes dedicated support and service-level agreements.

---

## 14. Does FlowForge provide an API?

Yes. FlowForge offers a developer API that allows teams to build custom integrations and automation pipelines.

---

## 15. Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. Subscriptions can be canceled at any time from the workspace billing settings.

---

## 16. Does FlowForge support large organizations?

Yes. The Enterprise plan provides advanced security, compliance features, and scalability for larger organizations.

---
